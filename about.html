About Us

What is the RMA?

The Roblox Music Associaition also known as the RMA is a music organization aiming to grow and organize the music education part of the Roblox Education (Ro-Ed) community. We provide promotion and resources for schools, events and competitions for Bands aswell as an outlet for people to promote their events and programs.
